I'm an AI researcher at Yutori.

I currently work on building Computer-Use Agent (CUA) models through mid-training and reinforcement learning, and most recently have been driving the development of Yutori Navigator.

My research interest lies in multimodal large language models and agentic systems, with an emphasis on long-horizon tasks and reliable evaluation in real-world settings.

Previously, I led post-training for LLaMA 3 Multimodal on the LLaMA team at Meta GenAI and co-led Emu, Meta's text-to-image foundation model.

I received my MS from Carnegie Mellon University.
